NEW RELEASE: "Simple" - Single and video

New song/video release, 28 October, 2022

ITS FINALLY OUT!

Hey Everyone!
The release of my new single "Simple" is finally out!!

I wrote this song about a relationship I had awhile back. It was that sort of relationship that had run it's course. Where our feelings for each other had faded somewhat and the inevitable end of the relationship was slowly dawning on us. The type of relationship that was so great but as time passed by we drifted apart until we were two strangers living together, loving each other for who we once where.

If you found the song relatable, send me a message with the story. Would love to hear from you guys!
Peace!

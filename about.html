About Gabriel

Born on December 8, 1992, in Stockholm (Sweden), Gabriel is best known for his soul and R&B inspired sound. Gabriel gained fame attending the thirteenth season of the popular Swedish Idol competition in 2017, in which he placed third overall. Since his departure from the program, Gabriel has garnered a vast following of fans and has since been releasing music with his label, Loco Diablo Productions.

Background

Gabriels's father originates from the Latin-American country of Uruguay and Gabriels mother originates from Sweden. Even though his upbringing was solely in Sweden he lets his heritage shine through in his music and style.

Gabriel comes from a family of musicians and grew up with recurrent guitar jam-parties around the dinner table with his grandfather, father, and uncles. His grandfather was a tango-singer from Uruguay and his dad had a salsa band in his younger days. Even though their musical careers had ended long ago, the music never stopped in the Cancela household.

Journey

From singing and writing music in his childhood room to 3rd place in Swedish idol to finally starting his own record label with his younger brother, Lu Ziano, who is Gabriel’s producer and a well-recognized rapper on his own. Gabriel is finally taking his own steps onto the music-scene.

With singles like “Simple”, “You Deserve Better” and “Sin Ti” continuously hitting the radio waves. There is no telling where his music-career might take him.
